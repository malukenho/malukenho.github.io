### Day 02: Red-Nosed Reports

The challenge itself was easy. But I've learned more about some C++ syntax,
in particular, one that I like a lot:

```cpp
auto my_function() -> int;
```

It reads way more natural to me than the traditional way:

```cpp
int my_function();
```
